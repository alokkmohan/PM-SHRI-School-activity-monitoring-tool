How It Works

Simple 4-step process for school principals to submit activity reports

1

Login with UDISE

Enter your 11-digit UDISE code. School details auto-fill from the master database.

2

Fill Activity Details

Enter activity name, date, and count of boys, girls, and teachers who participated.

3

Upload Photographs

Upload minimum 3 photos per activity directly from your phone or computer.

4

Submit & Done

Data saves to Google Sheets, photos organize to Drive. Instant confirmation.
